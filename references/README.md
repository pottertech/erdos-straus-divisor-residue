## Key Concepts

- **Kneser's theorem** — Additive combinatorics result for sumset lower bounds in abelian groups. Used in the Bounded Divisor-Residue Lemma.
- **Burgess bound** — Character sum bound giving least quadratic non-residue estimates. Used in Proposition 8.
- **Cauchy-Davenport theorem** — Special case of Kneser for Z/p with p prime.
- **Quadratic reciprocity** — Relates (p/q) and (q/p) for odd primes p, q. Used in Theorem 5.
